About Casa del Sol

Casa del Sol has been serving Austin authentic Mexican cuisine since 1987. Founded by the Hernandez family, we pride ourselves on traditional recipes, fresh daily-made salsas, and warm hospitality. Our vibrant atmosphere captures the spirit of Mexico with live mariachi music on weekends.
