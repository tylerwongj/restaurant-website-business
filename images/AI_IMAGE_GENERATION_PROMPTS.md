### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution**: 1920x1280px minimum (3:2 ratio)
- **Mobile Format**: 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio for social/mobile)  
- **Quality**: High resolution, commercial grade
- **Style**: Professional food/restaurant photography
- **Lighting**: Natural preferred, professional setup

### Consistent Style Prompts
Add these to every prompt:
- "professional food photography"
- "commercial quality" 
- "restaurant industry standard"
- "4K resolution"
- "natural lighting"

### File Naming Convention
```
category-subcategory-description-###.jpg

- customer-emotions-date-night-001.jpg
- food-trends-plant-based-002.jpg
- seasonal-spring-outdoor-003.jpg
- service-wine-presentation-004.jpg
- mobile-vertical-food-005.jpg
```
